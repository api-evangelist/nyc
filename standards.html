Standards

Two views. First, the open & industry data standards NYC agencies should conform to — most already exist for exactly what the city builds bespoke, and adoption is nearly zero. Second, the standards this project itself is built on — the design-first chain that turns 67 assessments into one coherent, agent-native model.

Dimension A

Standards NYC agencies should conform to

Sector standards mapped to the domains they apply to, with current conformance (✓ = at least partially live). The city keeps declining the standards made for its own problems.

Dimension B

The standards this project is built on

The modernization is defined by a small stack of open standards — one object contract, one API contract, an agent contract and its Agent Skills, plus discovery and the existing data source.
