# Dispute or appeal

*Common government process — Act · Dispute/File.* Part of the [NYC Programmable City](https://nyc.apievangelist.com/experience.html) experience layer.

**Challenge a city determination — appeal a property-tax assessment, dispute a violation or ticket, respond to a summons, file a grievance.**

## When to use

The resident is contesting a charge or decision. Identify the case, assemble the response against the agency's rules, and file the dispute/appeal.

## What it orchestrates

This skill spans **3 agencies** and **3 operations** across the corpus. It resolves the right agency for the task, then calls that agency's MCP tools (each backed by a REST operation) to complete it — anchoring any location on the [nyc-commons](https://nyc.apievangelist.com/commons.html) geography spine.

- [NYC Standards & Appeals (BSA)](https://nyc.apievangelist.com/domain.html?d=bsa)
- [NYC Admin Trials & Hearings (OATH)](https://nyc.apievangelist.com/domain.html?d=oath)
