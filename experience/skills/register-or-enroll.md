# Register or enroll

*Common government process — Act · Register.* Part of the [NYC Programmable City](https://nyc.apievangelist.com/experience.html) experience layer.

**Register or enroll with the city — register to vote or request a ballot, register a business or vendor, enroll in a program.**

## When to use

The resident is establishing a standing status. Collect the required identity/eligibility fields, register, and confirm.

## What it orchestrates

This skill spans **6 agencies** and **6 operations** across the corpus. It resolves the right agency for the task, then calls that agency's MCP tools (each backed by a REST operation) to complete it — anchoring any location on the [nyc-commons](https://nyc.apievangelist.com/commons.html) geography spine.
