# NYC Open Data — DEP Datasets

All NYC Open Data assets whose **Dataset-Information_Agency = "Department of Environmental Protection (DEP)"** (verified via the Socrata Discovery API, 2026-07-13). **57 assets**, sorted by lifetime page views. Machine-readable: [opendata-dep.json](opendata-dep.json).

The shape of the corpus is the story: it is **environmental telemetry and regulatory data, published generously but sprawling and inconsistently typed** — reservoir levels as cryptic SCADA tags, Harbor Water Quality as **100 columns of free text**, a dozen near-duplicate Watershed Water Quality tables, and parallel GIS layers with truncated shapefile column names. There is **no dataset for the customer service layer** (water-bill payment, account management, reporting a water-main break or sewer backup); that lives only in the login-walled uMAX **My DEP Account** portal or is funneled into generic NYC311. See [crosswalk.md](crosswalk.md).

## Groupings

- **Water supply / consumption:** Water Consumption in the City of New York (`ia2d-e54m`, DEP's single most-viewed asset), Current Reservoir Levels (`zkky-n5j3`, 25 cryptic SCADA columns).
- **Water quality:** Harbor Water Quality (`5uug-f49n`, **100 text columns**), Drinking Water Quality Distribution Monitoring (`bkwf-xfky`), Watershed Water Quality (`y43c-5n92`) and its ~dozen sub-tables (Hydrology, Limnology, Keypoint, Wastewater, Sites + matching Qualifier tables), Lead & Copper at-the-tap (`k5us-nav4`, `3wxk-qa8q`, `g63j-swsd`, `aa5e-digs`), Cryptosporidium & Giardia (`x2s6-6d2j`), Disinfection By-Products (`33c5-b922`).
- **Green infrastructure (stormwater):** Point Layer (`df32-vzax`), Regulated Projects (`fm4z-qud6`), Porous Pavement ROW (`n7f2-dyvt`), Medians (`drep-uzs7`) + matching map layers.
- **Distribution assets:** Citywide Hydrants map (`6pui-xhxz`, 2nd most-viewed) + Hydrants dataset (`5bgh-vtsn`), Citywide Catch Basins (`2w2g-fk3i`), Outfalls (`8rjn-kpsh`), Lead Service Line Location (`jqfp-uff7`).
- **Permits & compliance:** Clean Air Tracking System / CATS (`f4rp-2kvy`), Water & Sewer Permits current + historic (`hphy-6g7m`, `4k4u-823g`), Asbestos Control Program / ACP7 (`vq35-j9qm`) + Certified Asbestos Investigators (`m64p-r9hk`), Recreational Boating Permits (`idfb-y78n`), Significant Noncompliance List (`xnje-s6zf`).
- **Stormwater / flooding & wastewater:** Stormwater Flood Maps (`9i7c-xyvv`), Impervious Area GIS Study (`uex9-rfq8`), MS4 Data (`j57c-rqtq`), FloodNet sensors (`aq7i-eu5q`, `kb2e-tjy3`), Wastewater Treatment Plant Performance (`hgue-hj96`), Co-digestion / Biogas (`b3mq-yvvr`), Biosolids (`95kh-h6zc`).
- **Read-side tickets:** Work Order Management Module (`4fvw-nn9c`) — DEP's *completed* work-order/service-request records, the closest open twin to the locked service layer, but read-only and after the fact.
- **Other:** Recreation Area Maps (`8qgy-ka3v`), Land Acquisition Statistics (`39yy-hdfd`), Demand Management Projects (`7ppc-iii7`).
