The shared model

nyc-commons

One canonical definition of a borough, a BBL, an address, a place, a party, and a dollar — for all of NYC government. Across 67 domains the same objects are re-declared per agency; nyc-commons factors them into one versioned schema set, $ref'd from City Planning as the authoritative source. Resolve two records to the same BBL and they're provably about the same place.
