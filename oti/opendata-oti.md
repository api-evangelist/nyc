# NYC Open Data — OTI Datasets

All NYC Open Data assets whose **Dataset-Information_Agency = "Office of Technology and Innovation (OTI)"** (verified via the Socrata Discovery API, 2026-07-13). **221 assets** (114 datasets · 49 hrefs · 36 maps · 20 files · 2 filters), sorted by lifetime page views — the **top 50** shown here; all 221 with column schemas are in [opendata-oti.json](opendata-oti.json).

The shape of the corpus is the story: OTI is not a subject-matter agency, it is the **platform operator**, so its published corpus is a cross-section of the whole city — the **most-viewed dataset in NYC Open Data** (311 Service Requests, 1.26M+ views), the **catalog of the catalog** (LL251 Published Data Asset Inventory + Release Tracker), the entire **planimetric base map** (buildings, sidewalks, roadbed, elevation, land cover), **LinkNYC**, **municipal broadband**, **public Wi-Fi**, `.nyc` domains, algorithmic-tools and pay-transparency compliance reports, and NYC.gov web analytics. See [crosswalk.md](crosswalk.md).
