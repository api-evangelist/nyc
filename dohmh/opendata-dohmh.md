# NYC Open Data — Department of Health and Mental Hygiene (DOHMH)

All **81** datasets/assets published on [NYC Open Data](https://data.cityofnewyork.us) whose `Dataset-Information_Agency` metadata is exactly **"Department of Health and Mental Hygiene (DOHMH)"** (verified 2026-07-13 via the Socrata Discovery API). Sorted by lifetime page views — a proxy for public demand. Companion machine-readable file: [opendata-dohmh.json](opendata-dohmh.json).

DOHMH is one of the city's most-consumed publishers: its **Restaurant Inspection Results** is among the single most-viewed datasets in all of NYC Open Data. The famous set is fully open and machine-readable — the gap this assessment finds is not open data but **agent-native, transactional services** (see [crosswalk.md](crosswalk.md)).
