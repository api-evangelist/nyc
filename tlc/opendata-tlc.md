# NYC Open Data — Taxi & Limousine Commission (TLC) Datasets

All **80** assets on NYC Open Data (Socrata) whose `Dataset-Information_Agency` is exactly **"Taxi and Limousine Commission (TLC)"** (verified 2026-07-13 via the Socrata catalog API). Machine-readable copy: [opendata-tlc.json](opendata-tlc.json). Sorted by lifetime page views.

> Note: the TLC's flagship **Trip Record Data** (yellow/green/FHV/FHVHV) is published primarily as monthly **parquet files** on CloudFront (`d37ci6vzurychx.cloudfront.net/trip-data`), *separate* from Socrata. Only a subset of yearly trip snapshots is mirrored here. See [tech-stack.md](tech-stack.md) and [apis-observed.md](apis-observed.md).
