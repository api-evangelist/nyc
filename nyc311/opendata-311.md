# NYC Open Data — 311 Asset Index

The 311 dataset family on NYC Open Data (published by the **Office of Technology and Innovation (OTI)**), pulled 2026-07-13 — **15 datasets**. `311 Service Requests from 2020 to Present` (`erm2-nwe9`) is the flagship: **1.26M views, 590k downloads** — the single most-used dataset in this project, and one of the largest open datasets in the world. Full schemas in [opendata-311.json](opendata-311.json).
